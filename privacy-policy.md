# Privacy Policy

**Animated Weekday Wallpaper**  
Last updated: 27 March 2026

## Overview
Animated Weekday Wallpaper is a simple animated wallpaper app that displays the current day of the week on your screen.

## Data Collection
This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Local Storage
The app saves your preferences (colours, fonts, language) locally on your device only.

## Internet Access
Internet permission is used solely to load display fonts from Google Fonts on first launch.

## Third Party Services
Uses Google Fonts. See Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy
This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
